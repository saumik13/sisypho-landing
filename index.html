Describe, record, automate

With Sisypho, generate a skill for your computer to be able to do any repetitive work for you. In just a few simple steps, you describe work you have to do repetitively, show Sisypho a recording, and Sisypho will generate a skill for your computer.

01. Describe Your Task

Tell Sisypho what you want to automate in natural language

02. Record Your Actions

Show Sisypho how to perform the task by recording your screen*. Sisypho will outline what specific actions you have to perform in your recording.

*For browser recordings, make sure you have connected the extension to Sisypho

03. Test & Validate

Verify the automation works correctly with a test run. For any errors caught during validation run, Sisypho will try to fix the skill it generated by itself.
